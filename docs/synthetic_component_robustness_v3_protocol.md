# Frozen protocol: synthetic component-identification and non-floor robustness v3

Protocol `synthetic-component-identification-robustness-v3` is development-only and was frozen before any v3 outcome-producing run. The exact claim is limited to synthetic raw-sensor-assisted weak lexical/action grounding: in unbiased ambiguous event bags, synchronized training-only raw sensor streams improve cue-free held-out object-action composition accuracy for a non-oracular competitive compositional learner relative to both absent and matched shuffled streams.

The primary regime independently randomizes distractor primitive and manner relations subject to exact corpus-level balance. It forbids target duplication and surface-word or oracle fields in visible candidates. The old `(target + 3) mod 6` distractor is retained only in `adversarial_plus3_secondary` and is never pooled into primary inference.

The learner jointly accumulates repeated-occurrence evidence for primitive and manner tokens, normalizes candidates competitively within each slot, retains a null referent, and applies per-episode soft reliability derived solely from the fixed v2 detector's quality/availability outputs. It has no target pointer, semantic label, lexicon mapping, grounded flag, or evaluation feedback. Three stochastic initializations are averaged within each of 40 independent corpus seeds. The exact v2 implementation is retained as a frozen comparator.

Co-primary estimands are corpus-average synchronized-minus-absent and synchronized-minus-shuffled effects in the unbiased regime on cue-free held-out object-action compositions. The global conclusion uses an intersection-union rule. Each lift must be at least 5 points, its fixed-resample studentized-bootstrap 95% lower bound must exceed zero, and at least 30/40 corpus effects must be positive. Student-t intervals are sensitivity analyses. Sign and sign-flip tests are prohibited. Complete empirical distributions, zero masses, quartiles, and extrema are reported.

Transfer gates are synchronized primary and seen-combination accuracy at least 72%, structured held-out primitive×manner accuracy at least 62%, component accuracy at least 80%, aggregate manner pairwise-order accuracy at least 90%, and no more than 10% of primary model runs collapsed or inverted. The absent primary action control must be within 3 points of or above 1/6 chance; otherwise v3 is a design failure, regardless of sensor lift. Noun and zero-information lifts must remain within 5 points, corrupted channels may not be more than 5 points below absence, and oracle/direct-capacity controls must reach 90%/95%.

First-class diagnostics are primitive and manner pairwise-order accuracy, prototype margins, token collapse, inversion, active reliability distributions, model-seed performance, and the component-correctness/final-composition contingency. Secondary factor analyses cover candidate count, grounded rate, repetition, informativeness, SNR, dropout, false positives, visibility, and lag.

The final evaluator accepts only lexical prototypes plus cue-free action/object observations and candidate words. Raw streams, detector state, evidence, cues, and side encoders are structurally rejected. Truly zero-exposure words are evaluated separately and cannot be claimed learned.

All v1/v2 seeds and all v3 confirmation identifiers are blocked at generation, fitting, evaluation, reading, and summarization. Fixture identifiers alone are permitted before freeze. Exactly one frozen v3 development outcome run is allowed. No outcome-driven amendment is permitted, and no confirmation outcome is generated, read, inspected, or summarized.
